Design Improvement Project

My Design Improvement

For this project, I chose a design I thought I could improve on. Here are the problems I identified with the initial design:

  • It was cluttered
  • The color choices were bizarre
  • No sense of urgency

Here are the improvements I made:

  • Organized each missing item into its own distinguishable section
  • Improved color palette for better visual appeal, keeping all text black except making LOST be red and FOUND be green
  • Added warning with yellow background and black text to indicate urgency
